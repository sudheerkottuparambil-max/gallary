Twinmotion VR Presentation

The Twinmotion VR Presentation offers a fully immersive design walkthrough experience that transforms architectural and 3D models into an interactive virtual environment. Using Twinmotion’s real-time rendering and VR integration, this presentation allows clients to explore spaces at true scale — moving freely inside interiors, viewing lighting transitions, and experiencing realistic materials and ambience. By combining my expertise in 3D visualization, lighting composition, and virtual reality storytelling, I help clients and teams visualize the complete design intent before execution. This project highlights my ability to merge creativity with technology — delivering immersive, high-impact presentations that redefine design communication.
