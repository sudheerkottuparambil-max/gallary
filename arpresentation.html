Twinmotion AR Presentation

The Twinmotion AR Presentation showcases immersive architectural visualizations using Augmented Reality technology. This project brings 3D models to life, allowing clients to explore designs interactively in real-world environments through mobile and tablet AR view. By integrating Twinmotion’s real-time rendering with AR export features, I created a seamless experience that bridges the gap between concept and reality — enabling realistic lighting, material reflections, and spatial awareness. This presentation highlights my expertise in visual storytelling, real-time design communication, and client engagement through AR visualization.
