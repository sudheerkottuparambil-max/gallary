Blender – 3D Design & Visualization Tool

Blender is a powerful open-source 3D creation suite used for modeling, sculpting, animation, rendering, and visual effects. Proficient in creating high-quality 3D assets, realistic environments, and animated sequences, Blender allows designers to bring concepts to life with precision and creativity. Experienced in leveraging its advanced tools for texturing, lighting, rigging, and physics simulations to produce visually compelling projects for games, films, and interactive media.
